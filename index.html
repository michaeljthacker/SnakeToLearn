How to Play

Goal: Control the snake to eat red apples and grow as long as possible without hitting the walls or yourself!

Controls:

  • Desktop: Use W, A, S, D keys to move up, left, down, right
  • Mobile: Swipe in the direction you want to move
  • Start: Press any key or tap the screen to begin
  • Reset: Click the "Reset" button to start over

Difficulty: Higher numbers mean faster snake movement. Good luck!
